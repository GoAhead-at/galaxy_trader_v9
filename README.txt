GALAXYTRADER
================

Give your traders a brain. GalaxyTrader MK3 turns your freighters into intelligent,
self-managing traders that search for profitable routes, earn XP, attend training,
and handle repairs and equipment automatically.

Releases only on Nexusmods and Steam
Nexusmods: https://www.nexusmods.com/x4foundations/mods/1857
Steam: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3670908192

Full documentation: https://crapcode.dev/
